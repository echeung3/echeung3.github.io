  • Vitals:
  • HR < 40 or > 140
  • SBP < 80 or > 250
  • DBP<40 or DBP>130

  • Hb <5
  • Na < 120 or > 160

UCI Telemetry Orders
  • Acute Resp Failure (48 hr)
  • Chronic CHF (24 hr)
  • Acute PE (48 hr)
  • Sepsis (48 hr)
  • Cardiology/CT surgery (ongoing)
Review at 24-48 hr
  • Chest pain (low risk) - 24h
  • Syncope unknown etiology - 24h
  • Drug OD/Toxins - 24h
  • ACS / Chest Pain (High) - 48h
  • Stroke (cryptogenic) - 48h
Not Indicated
  • Chronic stable A-Fib
  • Anemia (stable)
  • GI Bleed (stable)
  • Febrile illness w/o shock
  • Stable PE w/o hemodynamic instability
