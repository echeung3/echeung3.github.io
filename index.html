Review at 24-48 hr
  • Chest pain (low risk) - 24h
  • Syncope unknown etiology - 24h
  • Drug OD/Toxins - 24h
  • ACS / Chest Pain (High) - 48h
  • Stroke (cryptogenic) - 48h
Not Indicated
  • Chronic stable A-Fib
  • Anemia (stable)
  • GI Bleed (stable)
  • Febrile illness w/o shock
  • Stable PE w/o hemodynamic instability
